Georgia Tech Glee Club

The Georgia Tech Glee Club formed as an all-male group in 1906, making it the oldest student organization on campus. Composed of graduate and undergraduate students, the members of the group represent all of the Institute's colleges. In recent years, the Glee Club has performed at prestigious events both at Georgia Tech and throughout the Atlanta community and has traveled to locations such as New York City, Hawaii, and the UK, spreading what it means to be a Ramblin' Wreck and a helluva engineer!
